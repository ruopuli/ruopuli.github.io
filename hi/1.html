HYPERSPACE

  DESTROYER

concept

The workshop combines the topic of speculative design and critical design and also related to the located realign of Bangrok. In the beginning, we have done some reacher about the history of this area and the Bangkok. We visited this area in order to actually feel the atmosphere, resident and problem. There are many problems in this place, and we decided to focus on the most interesting and also the confused one Dead end.

 

Amount of The dead end in this area is obviously too much for such a busy religion, which is really weird since it is an important commercial center and famous tourism spot. We started over thought the way how Bangkok ended up like this, interviewing some locals what’s the feeling about this sign and the reason why.

 

Every result and the history reach lead to the conclusion that people keep moving in and the urban planning wasn’t ready for all of them, that is the rapid growth of the population cannot follow the rate of city renewal which caused the imperfect of urban planning.

 

We based on the sign of “dead end” thinking that what kind of the people will have a chance to meet a dead end and what’s feeling and react they will have.

We choices the emotion that “want to get through the wall so badly” to become our start point. Based on this emotion, we made a kitting which is designed to face the dead end. The knitting is including a laser gun, a glass while using the gun, a putty and a glove for putty, and the instruction for all the objects in the box.

 

The setting of the gun is that the laser gun should be able to make a cool on the wall that can easily solve the dead end.

The putty is for the wall that had a hole on it, after user pass through the wall they have to fix the wall for the other.

The kitting will be put on the end of the dead end and the user will be tourism since the local resident have get used to the daily that full of dead end, the tourism is the only one that feel it is really unconvinced.

 

In this hypothetically situation , what we are tries to let the audience think of is:

If we can easily make a hole on the wall why should we ever fix it?

 

In this kitting for now, the object has actual use is the putty and the solve, why would we ever need something we don’t need?

 

Why the kitting is design for the tourism instead of the resident?

 

The concept of the gun is to leading people to start thinking about the area issue and what kind of the reason is behind the question?

 

What’s the history part and the ?No one ever wants to solve this problem why is that?

 

By pointing out this small question to make the public think of other questions of it, such like the reason behind dead end might be the growth of population and the road wasn’t enough which caused serious traffic jam and finally leading people to the main reason___the imperfect of urban planning.

The urban planning cannot fit to the rate of population is not only the problem in Bangkok but also in Bangkok, and a global issue as well. We don’t have a city that can catch up the rate .Though, we think the resident has the right to live in a more convince way instead of live with the problem day by day.

 

What we are saying to do is to make people start thinking “why is that”and further to encourage more people to take it serious and start to solve this problem.

We don’t have any solution or answer about how we are going to do with in our concept.

All we want to do is pointing out the question and leave a space for audience to discuss since there are too many to say.
